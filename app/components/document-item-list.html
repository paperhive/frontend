Knowledge Unlatched books

Knowledge Unlatched is a global consortium that promotes collaboration between libraries and publishers to make great academic books open access. The freed books are made available under a Creative Commons license. 106 books from 26 scholarly publishers have already been unlatched with the help of around 380 libraries plus consortia around the world. The full list of books which are being unlatched on an ongoing basis can be found on KU's website.
