Delete your account

Deleting your account is irreversible and likely affects other users too.

The following data is deleted or modified when you delete your account:

  • Your uploaded documents are deleted.
  • Your only me discussions are deleted.
  • Your contributions in private discussions are deleted.
  • Your document subscriptions are deleted.
  • You are removed from all channels and bookmarks in channels.
  • Channels are deactivated if you are the only owner.
Note that contributions to public discussions are not deleted.

Type DELETE to confirm.
