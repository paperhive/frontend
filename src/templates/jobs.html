About PaperHive

PaperHive has been developed for several months now and is fairly close to a preliminary release. Our founding team is based in Berlin and consists of André and Nico (mathematicians, computer scientists) and Alex (economist, business informatics). Several other people support us with optimization algorithms, web design and server management.

Job vacancies

Currently, we don't have an explicit job vacancy. However, if you share our enthusiasm for revolutionizing research communication and if you want to help us: please contact us!

Our software is built with AngularJS, node.js, MongoDB and Elasticsearch – make sure to let us know if you're experienced with any of these techniques. At PaperHive, we love free software (free as in freedom) and we contribute to the software we use and the free software ecosystem in general.

We strive for high diversity in our team and encourage you to get in touch with us regardless of your (and our) identity.
